CubeMaze

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-29

CubeMaze is an iOS / iPadOS puzzle game. We design it to collect the minimum data needed to provide gameplay, progress, Game Center, and purchase features.

Data we collect or process

  • Game Center player identifier and display alias, when you use leaderboards or achievements.
  • Gameplay progress, stars, coins, settings, random-mode preferences, and resume snapshots stored on your device.
  • Purchase history needed to deliver coin packs, restore purchases, and unlock Overview Pro through Apple StoreKit.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect advertising identifiers.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not share personal data with third parties for advertising.

Apple services

CubeMaze uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements, and Apple StoreKit for in-app purchases. These services are provided by Apple and may be subject to Apple’s privacy policy.

Storage

Gameplay progress and settings are stored locally with UserDefaults. Purchase and Game Center operations are handled through Apple APIs.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@cubemaze.app
