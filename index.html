Core gameplay

A 3D maze, understood as flat slices.

Move the slime on the visible 2D plane with one-finger swipes. Rotate the cube to reveal another slice of the same maze, then use the new perspective to reach the exit door.

1

Swipe on the visible face

The character moves only on the 2D grid you are currently looking at.

2

Rotate to change the slice

A wall in one view may become an open path after the cube turns.

3

Use perspective to reach the door

Plan across views and guide the slime into the exit.
